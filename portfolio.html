Combat Attorney
Working in Progress.

A battle/comedy comic, that I've drawn during higshchool.
It tells a story of an ex-attorney that is homeless and spends 99% of his free-time fishing in an alternate world of ours if the Hiroshima and Nagasaki bomb missed.
His weapon is a fishing rod and he relies on his creativity utilizing the fishing rod to defeat his foes.
