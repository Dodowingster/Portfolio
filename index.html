Welcome!

I'm Aqqil Bin Azman, my pen-name/alternate is Dodowingster (try clicking the banner to see him). I'm a passionate individual with a background in Software Engineering and a diploma in Game Development. Over the years, I've honed my creative skills and embarked on a journey of self-expression. This website serves as a platform to showcase my evolving body of work, which spans from my high school days to the current timeline.

What's unique about my art journey is that I have no formal education in art, yet I've embraced the world of creativity using a mix of free and paid software tools. I've ventured into various artistic realms, experimenting with different mediums and techniques. Join me on this creative odyssey as I share my artistic endeavors, each piece a testament to my passion and dedication.

I hope you find inspiration and enjoyment in exploring my work, and I'm excited to connect with fellow art enthusiasts and creators.
